Ancient Capital

Kyoto, the former imperial capital of Japan, is home to numerous classical Buddhist temples, gardens, imperial palaces, and traditional wooden houses.

Top Attractions:

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine - Famous for thousands of vermillion torii gates
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) - Stunning golden temple surrounded by gardens
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - Peaceful bamboo forest walk
  • Kiyomizu-dera - Historic temple with panoramic city views
  • Gion District - Traditional geisha district

Best Time to Visit:

Spring (March-May) for cherry blossoms or Fall (October-November) for autumn foliage
